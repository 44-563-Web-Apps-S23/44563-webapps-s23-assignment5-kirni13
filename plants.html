KingPalm

The King Palm Tree, scientific name Archontophoenix cunninghamiana, is one of the most popular palms in tropical and subtropical climates. The King palm looks very nice when planted in a grouping of 2 or 3. This sun-loving palm is mostly used outdoors, but it also makes an excellent house plant if given the right environment.

RainLilly

Zephryanthes is a genus of about 70 species in the Amaryllidaceae native to southern North America, Central America, and South America, with common names including rain flower, rain lily, and zephyr lily (Habranthus also has the common name rain lily).
